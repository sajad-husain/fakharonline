Our Export Excellence Story

From industry experience to entrepreneurial success, discover how Fakhar Iqbal Haider built Pakistan's trusted fruit export brand over 14 years of excellence

14 Years of Export Excellence

Fakhar Iqbal Haider's journey in the fruit export business began 14 years ago, following invaluable experience with renowned organizations including Shahpur Sugar Mills, Jauharabad Sugar Mills, Bab AlShams, and Anchor Kinnow. This diverse background provided deep insights into quality control, logistics, and international trade standards.

Drawing from this extensive experience, Fakhar Iqbal Haider established Fakhar Online with a clear vision: to bring the authentic taste of farm-fresh Pakistani mangoes and kinnow directly to customers' doorsteps. Today, we work with trusted partner farms across Punjab and Sindh, ensuring every fruit meets our stringent quality standards.

Quality & Sustainability Standards

Our commitment to quality and sustainability guides every aspect of our operations

Sustainable Sourcing

We partner with farms that use advanced water conservation techniques, including drip irrigation systems that reduce water consumption by up to 50% compared to traditional methods, ensuring sustainable farming practices.

Quality Partnerships

We work exclusively with farms that employ integrated pest management (IPM) techniques, using beneficial insects, crop rotation, and organic treatments to control pests naturally and reduce chemical usage by 80%.

Farm Standards

Our partner farms maintain soil health through organic composting, cover cropping, and natural fertilization methods. Regular soil testing ensures optimal nutrient balance for producing premium quality fruits.

Ecosystem Support

Our partner farms maintain diverse ecosystems with native plants and flowers that support local wildlife. This biodiversity creates natural balance and reduces the need for external interventions.

Farm Partnerships

We work closely with our partner farms, providing guidance on export standards, quality control, and sustainable practices. Our success is shared with the farming communities that support our mission.

Export Standards

Every fruit is carefully inspected and graded according to international export standards. Our 14 years of experience ensure that our products meet the highest quality and safety requirements for global markets.

Farm to Table Journey

Follow the journey of our fruits from orchard to your doorstep

1

Partner Farm Selection

We carefully select our partner farms based on strict quality criteria, sustainable farming practices, and their ability to meet our export standards. Each farm is regularly inspected to ensure consistent quality.

2

Perfect Harvest Timing

Fruits are harvested at the peak of ripeness, determined by sugar content, color, and texture. Our experienced farmers know exactly when each fruit reaches its optimal flavor and nutritional profile.

3

Quality Inspection

Every fruit undergoes rigorous quality checks for size, color, firmness, and absence of defects. Only fruits that meet our premium standards proceed to the next stage.

4

Expert Packaging

Fruits are carefully packed using eco-friendly materials designed to protect them during transit while maintaining optimal temperature and humidity conditions.

5

Door-to-Door Delivery

Our specialized logistics ensure fruits reach your doorstep within 24-48 hours of harvest, maintaining maximum freshness and nutritional value from our partner farms directly to your table.
